Who We Are
While living in cities across East Africa, meeting fascinating people, making friends, breaking bread and finding new colleagues, many entrusted me with their unique stories. They could have been inspiring TedX Talks about life, courage, joy and unexpected perspectives. Those cities were boiling with vibrant energy, exciting lifestyles and urban cultures. Yet, in the media world, those narratives are overshadowed by accounts of social unrest and political turmoils. For years, I was bombarded with one question: “How is Africa?” The gap between what I saw and what many imagined Africa to be was so striking that I realised it was time to act. I brought passionate individuals together to tell stories from African cities, offer tools to young people to tell theirs and bring harmony to all these different voices together in one single platform. Karibu, as they say in Swahili! Welcome.
— Liza Bell
