Digital Storytelling
How is Africa is teaming up with partners on the ground to deliver digital storytelling workshops for young people across Sub-Saharan parts of Africa.
Research shows that digital storytelling improves literacy and social interaction skills, empowers individuals and creates a strong sense of public culture.
We educate young people in digital storytelling skills so they are empowered to share their voices, stories and opinions and get global attention to their initiatives, businesses and perspectives.
